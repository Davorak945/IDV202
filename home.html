Craft Art Gallery is one of the largest privately owned galleries in Africa with a good reputation locally and internationally. The gallery specialises in South African artists and represent over 100 artist with 4000 artworks. The gallery has contemporary art, investment art and private collections that are carefully selected and then offered to corporate and private clients. Business is always evolving and so has our client needs regarding their art portfolios and support services we offer. Craft Art Gallery has recently expanded its exhibition space dramatically to allow for greater choice and to be considered a leader and one of the largest in Africa. We believe our customers are drawn to the gallery for its large variety of art displayed, and the ease we provide to our clients who prefer to buy online and the after sales service we offer all our clients. In addition Cafe d'Art offers breakfasts, lunches and high teas. The area is well designed and equipped with a play area for children that will also be launching later this year. Customer framing needs can be attended to in our workshop where we also have a school of framing for the creatives who desire to do framing themselves. We are very excited about Alice Art Gallery Online; it will offer full electronic commerce service with the best after sales service and advice possible with delivery worldwide. Although all the added services will enrich our customer experience our main focus is and always will be a satisfied client with both their purchase and the quality of experience during the process. We strive to always provide the ultimate experience in art, which is at the haert of the organisation. From humble beginnings in 1990, Craft Art has evolved significantly to become a recognised player in the industry for quality original art, advice, education and service to our customers. Customer relationships and their experience is paramount and at the core of the success of the business. Craft believes: "Art must be enjoyed and your portfolio must reflect who you are, your hopes and dreams and adapt to your life and taste"
